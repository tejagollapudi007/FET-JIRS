Welcome to our coding certification program page!

We are excited to offer a comprehensive coding curriculum to the students of Jain International Residential School, from the Faculty of Engineering and Technology, Jain (Deemed-to-be University). Our program is designed to introduce students to the world of computer programming, and provide them with the skills and knowledge they need to excel in the field of computer science.



Our curriculum is designed to be hands-on and interactive, with plenty of opportunities for students to practice their skills and build their own projects. We offer a wide range of courses, including AI, web development, mobile app development, and IoT. Our instructors are experienced professionals who are passionate about teaching and mentoring the next generation of coders.
